Shopped By Thread

An independent streetwear curator built around a single principle: every piece is brand new, hand-checked, and worth the price you pay for it.

The story

Shopped By Thread started where most good things in streetwear start — as a personal obsession. Sourcing rare drops, tracking release calendars, building relationships with the people who actually move pieces from production to your closet without anything getting watered down in between.

What was a private hunt became a quiet operation: moving authenticated, brand-new pieces from Hellstar, Denim Tears, Sp5der, Bape, Supreme, Chrome Hearts, and the labels that matter — to people who care about the difference between an actual piece and a knockoff.

No replicas. No "good condition." No stories. Just verified inventory from the brands shaping the culture.

What we carry

Every piece in our catalog is brand new with original tags attached. We don't sell pre-owned, vintage, or "deadstock" with vague provenance. If it's listed here, it's been hand-checked and arrives sealed.

Our roster shifts with culture. We focus on the labels that hold value beyond hype cycles — pieces from designers and houses that real collectors actually wear. When something drops that doesn't meet our standard, it doesn't get listed.

For wholesale

We work with boutiques, resellers, and stylists looking for verified inventory at scale. Wholesale clients get bulk pricing, priority on rare drops, and a direct line for sourcing requests.

Inside the inspection

Six markers counterfeits always get wrong.

→Stitching density. Authentic pieces have consistent stitch counts per inch. Replicas often have looser, uneven stitching especially around logos and seams.
→Hardware weight. Zippers, snaps, drawcord aglets — authentic hardware has noticeable weight and finish. Counterfeits feel hollow.
→Label placement. Inner labels are sewn at exact distances from seams on authentic pieces. We have reference measurements for every brand we carry.
→Print registration. Multi-color graphics need perfect alignment. Even a 1mm misregistration means it didn't come off an authentic press line.
→Fabric weight. Authentic pieces hit specific weights — Bape heavyweight tees are 7oz, Hellstar hoodies are 14oz. We weigh every garment.
→Security codes. Holographic stickers, RFID chips, woven security strips, hangtag QR codes — we verify these match each brand's current production batch.
The Shopped By Thread Guarantee

If it's not authentic, your money back. Period.

If any piece you receive doesn't match our authentication standard — for any reason — contact us within 7 days for a full refund and free return shipping. No fine print, no run-around.
